Introducing Frischh

  • A mobile platform which brings your favorite local grocery retail stores to your doorsteps on a Pay-as-you-go subscription model.
  • A single window access to all your favourite local stores which helps you save money and time spent on weekly grocery shopping.
  • Keep yourself informed about local promotions and optimise your grocery expenses based on intelligent expense optimisation advices.

As a Customer

  • Access to fresh grocery is a daily or weekly hassle, made of round trips across multiple local stores and long waiting queues.
  • A non-transparent market prevents consumers from easily comparing prices, products and availability across multiple local stores at a single glance.
  • No smart financial advisor exist to optimise your grocery expenses and consumers cannot know the local store promotions or offers unless they visit.

Key Features

  • Explore - Explore across multiple stores and setup your Frischh baskets with your favourite grocery items.
  • Subscribe - Subscribe to your Frischh baskets with flexible daily, weekly or monthly delivery frequencies.
  • Pay-as-you-go - Setup your Frischh wallet with the preferred payment method or top-up your wallet from bank account to pay your grocery bills monthly.
  • Pause - Pause, edit or cancel your Frischh basket subscriptions anytime you want, and your payment stops exactly at the same moment.
  • Optimize - Analyse your consumption every month and optimise your grocery expenses with adopting elegant promotion advices for your local stores.
